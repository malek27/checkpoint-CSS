what's a Panda ?

Pandas are a large bearlike mammals with characteristic black and white markings, native to certain mountain forests in China. It feeds almost entirely on bamboo and has become increasingly rare.. They are known for their cute and magistic morphology.





The main meal of pandas is Bamboo; a giant woody grass which is grown chiefly in the tropics.



Pandas live in a few mountain ranges in south central China, in Sichuan, Shaanxi and Gansu provinces. They once lived in lowland areas, but farming, forest clearing and other development now restrict pandas to the mountains.
